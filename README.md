## All Aggressive
**All Aggressive** is a hardcore survival mod that turns every mob in Minecraft into a deadly threat. Even the most peaceful creatures - cows, pigs, sheep, chickens - will now hunt you down and attack!
### ⚔️ Universal Hostility
Every mob in the game becomes aggressive towards players. No creature is safe to approach!
